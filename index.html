Deep knowledge across lighting direction, live visuals, stage engineering and software development gives a holistic view of production: not just how individual systems work, but how they interact and reinforce each other.

Whether you need a lighting programmer who reads the plot, or a developer who understands what an LD actually needs on the night - this is the one call to make.
